# NEON.RACER 🏎️⚡

A high-speed, multiplayer neon racing game inspired by the aesthetic of TRON. Dominate the grid, collect energy orbs, and outmaneuver your opponents in this real-time competitive arena.

## 🌟 Features

- **Real-time Multiplayer**: Compete against players from around the world in a seamless, low-latency environment.
- **Neon Aesthetics**: Immerse yourself in a vibrant, high-contrast world with glowing trails and sleek vehicle designs.
- **Dynamic Fuel Mechanic**: Manage your boost strategically. High-speed acceleration consumes fuel quickly, requiring careful timing to recharge.
- **Spatial Audio**: Experience the hum of the engines with spatial audio that reacts to your speed and proximity to other racers.
- **Leaderboard**: Climb the ranks by collecting orbs and surviving longer than your rivals.

## 🎮 Controls

- **A / D** or **Left / Right Arrow**: Steer your racer.
- **SPACE** or **W** or **Up Arrow**: Activate the Turbo Boost.
- **New Tab**: Open the game in a new window for a better experience.

## ⛽ Fuel & Boost Mechanics

- **Boost Duration**: A full fuel tank provides **2 seconds** of continuous boost.
- **Consumption**: Fuel is consumed rapidly while boosting.
- **Overheat**: If fuel reaches 0, the engine overheats (indicated by a **red bar**).
- **Recovery**: Fuel recharges automatically over **30 seconds**. You can boost again once the bar reaches 10% and turns **cyan** again.

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

- **Frontend**: React 19, Three.js (@react-three/fiber, @react-three/drei)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express, Socket.io
- **Post-processing**: Bloom effects for that signature neon glow.
- **Audio**: Web Audio API for synthesized engine sounds.

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. **Join the Arena**: Enter your name and hit "PLAY".
2. **Collect Orbs**: Drive over glowing orbs to increase your length and score.
3. **Avoid Collisions**: Don't hit the trails of other racers (or your own!).
4. **Master the Boost**: Use your turbo to cut off opponents or escape tight spots.
